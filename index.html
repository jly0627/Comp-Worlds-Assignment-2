A basic physics simulation with color-coded crates.

-Hold F while the mouse is near a crate to grab it and then move the mouse to drag it around.
-While dragging crates, collisions with platforms are disabled.
-Aiming with the mouse and pressing Q will create an invisible burst of force originating from the crate with the green circle.
-If a non-green crate collides with another with enough force their colors will change.
